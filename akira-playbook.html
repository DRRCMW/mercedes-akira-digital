What Changed in v2

The old playbook promised a mockup — "I even mocked something up for you." The new system delivers the finished demo before the first touch. FORGE builds a complete, compliant, personalized website for a hot lead in minutes, deployed to a live link. Every script below now sells from proof in hand, not a promise. This is the single biggest close-rate lever we have: the prospect isn't imagining a better website — they're looking at theirs.

The Demo-First Pipeline

1. Prospect Finder surfaces businesses with weak or missing sites → 2. Mercedes scores; Hot leads get the ⚒ Forge Mockup action → 3. FORGE builds the demo from a proven template (pick the matching niche template from your library — plumber lead gets your best plumber structure) → 4. Demo deploys to your demos repo (live URL) → 5. Outreach goes out with the live link in the first message → 6. SIGNAL turns the before/after into content → 7. Close, launch, review request at day 0 and day 30.

Rule of the system: no cold touch without a live demo link. The demo IS the pitch. Ten touches before noon means ten live demos working for you all day.

Objection Handling v2

"I already have a website."

"You do — and I looked at it before I built this one. Pull them up side by side on your phone. Which one would you call?" The demo makes this objection self-answering. Never argue; compare.

"How much?"

"The site you're looking at is the $1,000 Starter — done and live this week. Most [trade]s go Growth at $2,500 because it adds [FAQ, reviews, more service pages] which is what ranks on Google. Which look closer to what you want?" Always answer price directly, then anchor upward with a reason.

"I need to think about it."

"Totally fair. The link stays live for two weeks. One thing while you think: every day the old site is up, the guy across town is getting the calls that should be yours. When should I check back — Thursday or Friday?"

"Did you use AI to make this?"

"I use professional tools — same as every serious agency now. What you're judging is the result: does it look like the best [trade] in [City], and will it make your phone ring? The design system, the compliance, the strategy — that's me. The tools just make me fast, and my speed is your price."
